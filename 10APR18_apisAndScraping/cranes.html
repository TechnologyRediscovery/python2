Cable crane

Cable cranes allow for flexible movement of loads across large areas, like rivers and gorges

Backyard cable cranes

Cable is cheap, and is sold at all local hardware stores. You, too, could have a cable crane in your back yard with a few dollars and a winch!

H-Truss

Often built into industrial buildings for moving loads on an x-y plane projected on a workshop floor

Boom crane

Handy for mounting on trucks
